<p>
  	<strong>
      How to play
  	</strong>
</p>


<p>
    Select the ticket cost. Select any ten numbers or choose AUTO PICK then press PLAY or press the Space bar on your keyboard to purchase the ticket and start the game.
</p>


<p>
  	Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts.
</p>


<p>
  	At the end of the game, any winnings for that go are displayed in the TOTAL WON panel. To play again press the PLAY button or press the Space bar on your keyboard.
</p>


<p>
    Press PLAY and twenty winning numbers will be selected. Some numbers may reveal bonus stars. Press REVEAL ALL to skip to the result. Match five to ten winning numbers, or zero to win the corresponding prize.
</p>
